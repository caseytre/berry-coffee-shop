Our Story

Small shop, big intention.

Berry started as a simple idea: a place where the coffee is good, the space is calm, and nobody is rushing you out the door. We opened in 2024 with a small menu, a few cozy tables, and a belief that the best moments happen when you actually stop and sit.

We source our beans from small farms, keep our menu tight, and change our seasonal drinks based on what feels right. Nothing fancy, just thoughtful.

What we care about

  • Good ingredients, sourced carefully and used simply.
  • A space that feels like yours, not ours.
  • Slow mornings and unhurried afternoons.
  • Drinks made with care, every single time.
